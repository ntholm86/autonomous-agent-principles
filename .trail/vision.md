## What this repo is

The authoritative statement of three principles for trustworthy human-AI delegation — **Commander's Intent, Observable Autonomy, Convergence Is Silence** — authored by Nils Holmager, intended to stand independently of any implementation.

## What it is not

It is not a framework. It is not a tool. It does not prescribe how to build. Any system — skill suite, execution harness, organizational process — can conform or fail to conform to these principles, and that testability is the point.

## The destination

These principles should be as citable, as named, and as domain-agnostic as SOLID. The skills suite (`autonomous-agent-skills`) is one conformance example in one domain (developer tooling); it is evidence, not the proof.
